What redemption means

Redemption is the mechanism by which a holder requests settlement against the COSIGO silver anchor under the applicable contract rules, fulfillment terms, thresholds, and delivery method.

Redemption Guidance

How to redeem

Before you start

  1. Make sure you hold COSIGO tokens in your wallet.
  2. Make sure you have a small amount of ETH for network fees.
  3. This interface is intended for Ethereum mainnet (chainId 1).
  4. Confirm the official COSIGO contract address matches:
    0x0f1e8ee8a035270ed9952591d7dbdc600e2b4a49
  5. Use your wallet browser when possible for the smoothest connection flow.

Step-by-step

  1. Open this page inside your wallet browser or connect your wallet.
  2. Enter the redemption amount in milligrams (mg).
  3. Select your preferred fulfillment method.
  4. Press Preview Redemption to review the request.
  5. Press Connect & Redeem to initiate the on-chain action.
  6. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.
  7. Retain the transaction hash for fulfillment and recordkeeping.
