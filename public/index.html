How to redeem (phone + wallet browser)
Before you start
  1. Make sure you have Cosigo tokens in your wallet.
  2. Make sure you have a small amount of ETH for network fees.
  3. This page works on Ethereum mainnet (chainId 1).
Step-by-step
  1. Open this page inside your wallet browser (recommended).
  2. Enter the amount you want to redeem in milligrams (mg).
  3. Select your delivery method.
  4. Tap Preview to see fees.
  5. Tap Connect & Redeem.
  6. Confirm transaction in wallet.
  7. Wait for tx hash confirmation.
Important: This action sends an on-chain transaction. Tokens are burned according to the contract rules. Always verify you are on redeem.cosigo.io and the contract address.
